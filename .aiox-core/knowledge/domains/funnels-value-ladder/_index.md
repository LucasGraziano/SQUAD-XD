## Core Convergences
- First funnel's job is NOT profit - it's customer acquisition (breakeven first)
- Ascension is the profit engine (Value Ladder: bait > frontend > high ticket > continuity)
- Pre-frame determines conversion more than sales page itself
- Funnels are modular (Lego-like), not artisanal one-offs
- Upsell sequencing is non-negotiable (35-55% contribution benchmark)

## Key Metrics
- Breakeven: CPA <= front-end price
- Upsell contribution: 35-40% good, 55% top, <20% = broken
- $/subscriber/month: $1 benchmark
- Webinar close rate: 5-10% without Stack, 20%+ with Stack
- Invisible Funnel: 85% attendance vs 30% for free
- Pre-sell passage: 60-70% good, >70% top
