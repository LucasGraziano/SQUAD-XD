# Systems & Ops Knowledge Domain

## Experts
- **Sam Oven** - Offer design (Bridge Analogy), Purple Ocean positioning, business archetypes
- **The Scalable Company (Ryan Deiss)** - ScalableOS, playbooks, value engines, meeting rhythms
- **David Allen** - GTD, 6 Horizons of Focus, Natural Planning, action selection
- **Ken Schwaber** - Scrum, Inspect-and-Adapt cycles, role separation

## Core Convergences
- Systems scale, people don't (TSC + Oven + Allen)
- Document before hiring - chaos + new hires = more chaos (TSC + Linder)
- Simplicity is prerequisite for scale (TSC: "napkin test", Oven: 20/80)
- Feedback loops at multiple frequencies (Schwaber: daily/sprint/retro, TSC: weekly/monthly/quarterly)
- Bottom-up clarity enables top-down strategy (Allen: clear runway first, TSC: map how customers happen)

## Key Frameworks
- Bridge Analogy: Current State > Bridge (Offer) > Desired State (Oven)
- Purple Ocean: Red + Blue = differentiated in proven market (Oven)
- ScalableOS: BUILD 6-8wk > INSTALL 2-3wk > OPTIMIZE ongoing (TSC)
- 6 Horizons of Focus: Runway > Projects > Areas > Goals > Vision > Purpose (Allen)
- Inspect-and-Adapt 3 Levels: Daily > Sprint > Retrospective (Schwaber)
