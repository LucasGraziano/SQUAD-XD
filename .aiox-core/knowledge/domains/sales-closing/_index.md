# Sales Closing Knowledge Domain

## Experts
- **Jeremy Miner** - NEPQ methodology, self-persuasion, tonality mastery
- **Cole Gordon** - 7 Beliefs Framework, team scaling, call flow systems

## Core Convergences
- Objections = failed discovery, not failed pitch (both agree)
- Self-persuasion > external pressure (Miner: "Why do you feel?", Gordon: internal pressure)
- Tonality matters more than words (Miner: 72% non-verbal, Gordon: 7-38-55 Mehrabian)
- Prevention upstream > handling downstream (both)
- Discovery is where the sale happens, not the close

## Key Metrics
- Problem Awareness: 10-15 min (Miner) = 80-90% of the sale
- Full NEPQ call: 35-55 min (Miner)
- Close rate healthy: 20-50% (Gordon), >80% = underpricing
- Pitch check-in: every 45s (Gordon)
- Follow-up window: 24-48h (both)
- Booking window: 2-day rolling max (Gordon)
- Show rate: 60-65% to 85%+ with confirmation page optimization (Gordon)
