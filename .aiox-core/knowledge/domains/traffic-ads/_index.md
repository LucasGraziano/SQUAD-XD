## Core Convergences
- Creative volume is the #1 competitive advantage (Haynes: 50+/week Tornado; Mark: 20/hora com AI)
- Research first, always — foundational docs (Mark) and customer voice (Mark, Doug) before any creative
- Contrast marketing wins saturated markets — market sophistication stages (Schwartz) + Zero to One (Mark)
- DSL > VSL: 77% reach slide 5 vs 5-9% press play on video (Haynes)
- Show rate optimization is a system, not a single tactic (Hammer Strategy — Haynes)
- 80/20 testing: 80% iterations of proven winners + 20% new concepts (Mark)
- Quiz funnels prime emotionally before the offer — micro-commitments + seeding + pre-handling (Mark)
- Identity is the foundation: broke identity blocks all tactics before they start (Mark)

## Key Metrics
- 50+ creatives/week minimum (Haynes, Tornado era)
- 20 AI image creatives per hour with foundational docs workflow (Mark)
- 80/20 test split: 80% winner iterations, 20% new concepts (Mark)
- Image ad fatigue: only concern after $10K-$50K spend per creative (Mark)
- Hit rate <10% is normal - increase volume, not refinement
- Kill losers in 24-48h, scale winners immediately (Haynes)
- 12 emails + 4 SMS in 48h = 50-80% show rate (Hammer Strategy — Haynes)
- 25% watch time = move lead to next funnel step (Haynes)
- Quiz: never more than 2-3 questions in same format consecutively (Mark)
- Income qualification: 5-10x service price minimum (Haynes)
