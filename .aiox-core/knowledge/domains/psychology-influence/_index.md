# Psychology & Influence Knowledge Domain

## Experts
- **Blair Warren** - One Sentence Persuasion, Self-Verification Theory, Permission Paradox
- **Hard Copy (Kleiver)** - Subtexto, sentimentalismo, amnesia cognitiva, verdade como arma, curiosity gap 2+2, arco emocional Kinshu

## Core Principles
- Understanding > praising. People prefer negative feedback consistent with self-image over positive feedback that isn't
- Validating an emotion reduces its restrictive power (Permission Paradox)
- The deepest desire is to be UNDERSTOOD, not complimented
- Resistance comes from repression, not expression

## Application to Sales
- In discovery: mirror the prospect's self-perception BEFORE proposing change
- Before asking for action: give explicit permission for fear/doubt to exist
- "It's normal to be scared of this" disarms resistance more than any argument
- Demonstrate you UNDERSTAND the situation better than they can articulate it
