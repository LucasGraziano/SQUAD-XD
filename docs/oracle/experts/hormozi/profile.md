## Acquisition.com Model
- **Structure:** Minority equity stakes (20-35%) in established businesses
- **Differentiators:** Expertise-first (not just capital), founders keep control, profit-focused, no forced timelines
- **Ideal portfolio company:** $1M-$10M+ revenue, already profitable, service-based/SaaS/education, coachable founder
- **Value-add:** Offer optimization, lead gen systems, sales process, talent, operational efficiency, brand leverage
- **NOT looking for:** Pre-revenue startups, heavily regulated industries, massive capex businesses
